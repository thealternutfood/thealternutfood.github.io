About Us

"Cnóchi" pronounced k-no-chi is derived from "Cnó" the Irish for nut and "Chi" from cheese. The name gives praise to its ingredients and its Irish roots, born and bread in Dublin City. Made from premium grade cashew nut milk which is cultured and aged with the live bacteria to create a cheese like product. Dairy free and plant-based makes it an interesting addition to your cheese board for vegans and non-vegans alike.
